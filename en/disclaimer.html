What this app is

AstroGuide is a piece of consumer entertainment software in the long tradition of astrological reflection. It uses real astronomical data and language models to produce personal, readable text about the symbolic positions of planets at the time of your birth and at the present moment.

It is intended to be useful in the same way that a quiet conversation with a thoughtful friend is useful: as something to think with, not something to obey.

What this app is not

  • It is not a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, or prevent any condition. If you are unwell — physically or mentally — please talk to a qualified medical professional. Your therapist is more useful than your Saturn return.
  • It is not financial advice. Don't make investment, business, or money decisions on the basis of a transit. Talk to a licensed advisor.
  • It is not legal advice. If you are in a legal situation, talk to a lawyer.
  • It is not a prediction service. The texts describe symbolism, not certainty. No specific future event is being foretold.
  • It is not a substitute for human judgement. The chart is a mirror; the choices are yours.

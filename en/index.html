FULL TOUR

More inside the rooms.

The screens you find as you move through the app — what they look like, what they actually do. All of them are live: tap, scroll, navigate. Treat it as the demo before the demo.

01

The welcome ritual

First launch is a six-second pause: a silent welcome video, a single soft chime fading in and out, and the sigil settling on screen. The whole point is to remind you this isn't a notification feed — it's a small daily ceremony.

02

Setting up your chart

One scrollable form, no wizard, no "next" buttons. Name, gender, birth date, time (with an honest "I'm not sure" option), and place. Behind it the sigil rotates and the field slowly fills with stars. We tell you exactly which parts of the chart are time-sensitive so you can decide if you want to track down your birth certificate.

03

No chart? Still works.

Don't want to enter birth data? Pick a sun sign and the whole app runs in sign-mode — horoscope, transit days, even synastry by sign. You can switch to a full natal chart later and everything you've journaled comes with you. The free tier is real.

04

Weekly & monthly forecasts

Same anatomy as the daily — lead, summary, mood, love, career, ritual — but written for a wider arc. Weekly highlights the aspect that defines the seven days; monthly tracks where the Moon's cycle meets the slower outer-planet movements. Tap the period chip to switch between them.

05

Reading for someone else

Curious about a friend's sun sign or want to read a partner's day before yours? The "forecast for another sign" flow keeps your own profile intact and gives you a one-tap read for any of the twelve signs. Nothing in your data changes.

06

Past entries, your handwriting

Every moon journal entry stays available — dated, prompted, and quietly arranged on its own screen. There's no streak counter, no shaming notification if you skip a phase. The point is to come back when you want, not when an algorithm needs your attention.

07

Three-card spread

Past, present, future — but drawn from real Rider-Waite-Smith images and read as symbolism, not certainty. Tap each card to flip it, see its title, and read a short line. The full meanings unfold underneath, with reversed positions called out and explained.

08

Settings — short on purpose

Language (English, Russian, Spanish), the subscription status, app version, the legal documents, and a clearly labeled delete account. No dark patterns, no notification permission grabs disguised as features.

QUESTIONS

Questions people ask
before the magic works.

Is AstroGuide free?
Yes. The Android app launches on Google Play in May 2026 with a real free tier: daily horoscope, full natal birth chart, basic moon ritual, and sun-sign compatibility. Premium adds month-ahead transits, detailed synastry, and the full tarot oracle. One subscription, one price, no per-feature unlock games.
Do you really calculate the chart, or is it just AI guessing?
We really calculate. AstroGuide uses Swiss Ephemeris — the same astronomical engine professional astrology software has used for decades. The chart geometry is fixed before any language model sees it. The magic is anchored to math; the AI's job is to find the sentence that makes it land.
So... is this magic or psychology?
Both, in the way good astrology has always been both: symbol, timing, story, and self-recognition. AstroGuide does not guarantee future events or give medical, legal, or financial advice; it gives you a magical frame for noticing what is already moving.
Where is my birth data stored? Is it sold?
Your data stays on your device by default. If you choose to back up to the cloud, it's stored encrypted and tied only to your account. We don't sell it. We don't share it with advertisers. See Privacy.
Why do you ask for birth time?
The rising sign (Ascendant), Midheaven, and house positions move quickly — a four-minute difference can change them noticeably. If you don't know your exact time, you can skip it; the chart still works, and we'll show you which parts are time-dependent.
How do I delete my account?
From inside the app: Settings → About → Delete account. Or from this site: Account deletion. Deletion is permanent and removes your chart and journal entries within thirty days.
Does it work offline?
Your chart, daily horoscope, moon journal, and tarot deck all work offline once generated. New synastry calculations and fresh forecasts require a connection — we compute them on our servers and stream the prose back to you.
